WHAT'S INSIDE

Every stage of an asset's life, in one place

From the moment a laptop is tagged to the day it's retired, AssetFlow keeps a single, trustworthy record — so nobody has to ask "who has this right now?" in a hallway.

AF-DASH

Dashboard

Live KPI cards for available, allocated and overdue assets, plus one-tap actions to register, book or raise a request.

AF-ORG

Organization setup

Admins manage departments, asset categories and the employee directory — the only place roles get assigned.

AF-REG

Asset registry

Auto-tagged assets (AF-0001, AF-0002…) searchable by tag, serial number, category, status or location.

AF-ALLOC

Allocation & transfer

Assign an asset to a person or department, with a built-in rule that blocks double allocation and offers a transfer instead.

AF-BOOK

Resource booking

Calendar-based booking for shared rooms and equipment, with automatic overlap rejection.

AF-MAINT

Maintenance

Repair requests move through Pending → Approved → Assigned → In Progress → Resolved, updating asset status automatically.

AF-AUDIT

Asset audit

Run scheduled verification cycles; auditors mark items Verified, Missing or Damaged, with discrepancies flagged automatically.

AF-RPT

Reports & analytics

Utilization trends, maintenance frequency and booking heatmaps, exportable for leadership review.

AF-LOG

Activity & notifications

Every allocation, approval and overdue return lands in a searchable, timestamped log — nothing happens off the record.

THE CORE RULE

You can't allocate what's already taken

This is the rule the rest of the system is built around — every allocation attempt is checked against current holders before it's approved.

Conflict-aware allocation

Priya already holds Laptop AF-0114. When Raj tries to claim the same asset, AssetFlow blocks the request, shows exactly who holds it, and offers a transfer instead of a silent failure.

  • ✓ Current holder shown immediately, no guessing
  • ✓ Transfer requests route through Approved → Re-allocated
  • ✓ Allocation history kept per asset, forever
